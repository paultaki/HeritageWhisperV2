# Security Vulnerability Disclosure

Thank you for helping keep HeritageWhisper and our users safe. We appreciate your efforts to responsibly disclose security vulnerabilities.

## Reporting Process

1. Email security@heritagewhisper.com with details of the vulnerability
2. Include steps to reproduce, potential impact, and any proof-of-concept code
3. Allow up to 48 hours for initial response
4. Do not publicly disclose the vulnerability until we have addressed it

## What to Expect

- We will acknowledge receipt within 48 hours
- We will provide a timeline for addressing the vulnerability
- We will work with you on coordinated disclosure timing
- We will credit you for the discovery (unless you prefer to remain anonymous)

## Out of Scope

- Social engineering attacks
- Physical attacks against HeritageWhisper infrastructure
- Attacks requiring access to a user's device or credentials
- Denial of service attacks

## Safe Harbor

We support safe harbor for security researchers who:
- Make a good faith effort to avoid privacy violations and data destruction
- Report vulnerabilities privately and allow reasonable time for patching
- Do not exploit vulnerabilities beyond what is necessary to demonstrate the issue
